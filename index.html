Choose the feel

Different sessions can lean in different directions — on purpose.

The style is flexible, but not random. These are the main visual lanes the work can move through depending on the person, location, and reason for the session.

Style 01

Moody

Richer shadows, stronger contrast, and a more dramatic atmosphere for portraits that need emotional weight.

Style 02

Bright & airy

Soft, open, and light-filled. Great for family sessions, spring shoots, and images that should feel fresh and optimistic.

Style 03

Documentary

More movement, more in-between moments, and less over-posing — ideal when the real interactions matter most.

Style 04

Editorial

Cleaner compositions, stronger styling, and a refined finish for portraits that need a more elevated visual edge.
