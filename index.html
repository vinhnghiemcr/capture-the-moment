ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Nghiem. I believe every moment deserves to be remembered beautifully.

I'm a photographer passionate about capturing authentic emotions and genuine connections. Whether it's the joy of graduation, the warmth of family bonds, or the excitement of a special event, I strive to document these fleeting moments with artistry and care.

My approach is versatile - from moody and dramatic to bright and airy, documentary-style candids to polished editorial shots. Every session is unique, and I adapt my style to match your vision and personality.
